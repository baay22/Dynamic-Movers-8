ALL ABOUT MOVING PIANOS – THE DOS AND DON’TS

Moving can be a very stressful activity. This is especially when you are moving from one place to another and do not have assistance in moving their personal belongings. If you have a piano at home, the move can be especially stressful because in addition to being bulky, moving a piano requires special skills and expertise. In this post, we are going to look at the factors that are involved in moving a piano so that you can understand the process better. Also, we will look at the factors to consider when choosing the right cheap movers nyc in your area.

Consider hiring piano movers

    If you are thinking of having an easy piano move from home, you should consider hiring a professional moving company. Fortunately, there are many piano moving companies out there who can help you with the move. The movers can handle any type of a piano move whether it is local or a long distance move, efficiently and professionally. The professional will ensure that your grand piano and its furniture get to the desired destination in perfect condition. Hiring a piano moving company for the move can pay off as opposed to asking friends and family for help. Professional moving companies come with insurance covers and what this means is that if something breaks, they will compensate you. Below are the reasons why you should consider professional movers.

The right kind of equipment

    Professional moving companies are preferred by many people because they guarantee a safe move. This is especially considering that these companies come with specialized equipment and trucks that help with the packaging, loading and moving of expensive pans and furniture. The companies have the necessary skills to handle interstate and long distance travels that require large trucks to handle large loads. By looking around, you will find good piano moving companies that help move large loads for much less.

High level of experience

    Your move is special and unique. Therefore, it is important to find a mover who uses different methodologies for the move. A professional moving company has a methodology that provides for any type of piano. No matter where you are moving to, good movers will do what they can to make your moving as smooth as possible. Cross country moves can especially be complex and thus, it is wise to use the services of a professional. The good thing is that professional movers have the capacity and experience to affect this move smoothly and professionally.

Affordable rates

    In addition to moving pricy pianos and furniture efficiently, the right moving companies will give you affordable rates for the services. To get the best deal out of a moving company, it is advisable to compare different moving quotes on the market. However, it is important to note that piano moving quotes are often facilitated based on the individual needs of the customer. Although gas prices are rising by the day, you can save on the expenses by hiring a mover for just a fraction of the cost. But if you cannot afford full cost, many of the piano movers will be able to offer you with a partial service. For example, the professionals can let you handle the packaging while leaving you with the rest. You can also find companies that rent out their trucks, saving you 25% of the cost.

How pianos are moved

    Before we look at how much do piano movers cost, it is important to consider how pianos are moved. When you hire a professional company to move your piano, you should expect to have 2 to three people to come to your premises equipped with piano moving pads, skids, slings, ramps, and proper knowledge on how to move pianos safely. The professionals will also use special skills to move the piano out of your house. And when the piano is safely loaded on the truck, it will be fastened securely to avoid damage during transport. In the most difficult cases, 4 to 5 people may be required for the move.

    It is important to note that when a piano is not moved by a professional, proper caution will not be taken and you risk damaging your expensive equipment. And although hiring a professional mover does not completely eliminate the risk of damage, it greatly reduces it. The good thing is that the professional will assess the risk of the move and in the process advise you on whether it is wise or safe to proceed or not.

The costs

    How much do piano movers cost? Piano movers consider many factors when determining the costs for moving a piano. This can be the size and type of a piano, the furniture accompanying the piano as well as the distance to be traveled. Also, consider the quality of the service vis-à-vis the price. It will do you no good to hire a cheap piano mover only to get low quality services and risk damaging your expensive piano during transportation.

Conclusion

    Above, we have looked at how much do piano movers cost. However, it is important to note that different piano transportation services vary in the quality of service offered as well as the experience and this may affect the kind of service that you receive.
